Frequently asked questions

Is this available in my city or county yet? ›

We’re starting with select Florida jurisdictions and expanding based on early access demand. In early access, you can tell us where you work most and we’ll prioritize onboarding those forms first.

Do you sign or seal anything for me? ›

No. PermitBot Pro is a document automation tool, not a design professional. You retain full responsibility for reviewing, sealing, and signing any forms that require professional certification or authorization.

Can this submit directly to agency portals? ›

Our first version focuses on generating clean, complete packets. Direct portal integrations for select agencies are on our roadmap and will be rolled out with firm and enterprise tiers.

Is my data secure? ›

All data will be encrypted in transit and at rest. We’ll also offer firm-level controls for retention, deletion, and user roles so you stay compliant with your internal QA/QC procedures.
